In Wallet, your money can be stored in several wallets with the principle of "one wallet, one currency". That means you can't transfer money from one wallet to another or convert all your funds to one currency.

![](public://attachments/091bf854e97da92eb3853fe258e72061)

If the Wallet currency differs from the currency of the selected withdrawal method, the funds will be automatically converted into the withdrawal method currency.

Conversion may be unavailable for some currency pairs.

The Wallet exchange rate is shown on the payment request page. Please note: It may differ from the rates in exchange services.

If you want to withdraw money without converting it, use the withdrawal method whose currency matches your Wallet account's currency.

### Specifics of converting Brazilian reals (BRL) and Mexican Pesos (MXN)

If you withdraw funds from a BRL- or MXN-denominated wallet with conversion to other currencies, operational taxes will be charged when you create a payment request.

Tax amount:

- 25% for transfers from a BRL-denominated wallet with conversion to other currencies
- 25% for transfers from a MXN-denominated wallet with conversion to other currencies

The tax amount charged will be indicated in the **International transaction fee** item of the invoice.

The tax amount is affected by the exchange rates for BRL and MXN to these currencies and the high cost of servicing international transactions.
