   1. Sub-step A
   2. Sub-step B
2. Step Two

## Three Levels Deep (Numbered + Bullets)

1. Step One
   - Sub-item A
     - Sub-sub-item 1
     - Sub-sub-item 2
   - Sub-item B
2. Step Two
   - Sub-item C
     - Sub-sub-item 3
     - Sub-sub-item 4

## Three Levels Deep (Bulleted + Numbers)

- Item One
  1. Step A
     1. Sub-step 1
     2. Sub-step 2
  2. Step B
- Item Two
  1. Step C
     1. Sub-step 3
     2. Sub-step 4

## Deeply Nested (Four Levels)

1. Step One
   - Sub-item A
     1. Sub-step 1
        - Sub-detail a
        - Sub-detail b
     2. Sub-step 2
   - Sub-item B
2. Step Two
   - Sub-item C
     1. Sub-step 3
        - Sub-detail c
        - Sub-detail d
     2. Sub-step 4
