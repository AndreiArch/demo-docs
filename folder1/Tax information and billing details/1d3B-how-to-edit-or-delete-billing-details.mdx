You can only edit the billing details with the **Editing&#x20;****required** status.

1. Click ![](public://attachments/7d62f05b18482c76d09061ffb6f28b59) to the right of the status.
2. Select **Edit** and correct the errors.

To delete billing details, do the following:

1. Click ![](public://attachments/7d62f05b18482c76d09061ffb6f28b59) to the right of the status. 
2. Select **Delete**.
