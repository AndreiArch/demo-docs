    <td align="left">
      **Overview snapshot tile**
    </td>
    <td align="left">
      This tile displays campaign metrics based on the time interval you select. <font color="#111010">Time intervals</font>:&#x20;
      - **Lifetime**: Displays data from the start of the campaign through yesterday.&#x20;
      - **Yesterday**: Displays data for yesterday, ending at midnight.&#x20;
      - **Today**: Displays data for today, starting at midnight.
        **Metrics**:
      - **Impressions**: Total impressions won during the time interval selected.
      - **CPM**: Average CPM based on the time interval selected.&#x20;
        - CPM means cost per 1,000 impressions&#x20;
        - Formula: (total spend ÷ number of impressions) × 1,000 = CPM
        - Example: ($40,718 ÷ 9,616,479) \* 1,000 = $4.23 CPM
      - **Win Rate (%)**: Average of impressions won compared to bids sent during the time interval selected.
        - Formula: impressions won ÷ bids sent = win rate
        - Example: 10,000 impressions won ÷ 100,000 bids sent = 10% win rate
      - **KPI 1 - Cost Per Action (USD)**: Average of KPIs during the time interval selected.&#x20;
      - **CTR (%)**: Clickthrough rate. This metric is only displayed if your campaign has at least one line item with a display, native, or DOOH channel. Otherwise, VCR (%) is displayed.&#x20;
        - Formula: (clicks ÷ impressions) \* 100 = CTR % &#x20;
        - Example: (12,075 ÷ 9,616,479) \* 100 = .13%
      - **VCR (%)**: Video completion rate. This metric is only displayed if your campaign has at least one line item with a video, CTV, or audio channel. Otherwise, CTR (%) is displayed. &#x20;
        - Formula: (number of viewers who completed the video ÷ total viewers who started the video) \* 100 = VCR&#x20;
        - Example: (850 ÷ 2,000) \* 100 = 42.5% VCR
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td align="left">
      **Pacing snapshot tile**
    </td>
    <td align="left">
      You can toggle between Current Flight and Daily.&#x20;
      **Current Flight**:&#x20;
      - **Goals**: If you're pacing towards Impressions, the goal will be the Flight Impression Goal. Towards Spend, the goal is the Flight Spend Cap.
      - **Spent**: Total spent so far (not counting today)
      - **Amount at Risk**: Pacing performance.&#x20;
        - Formula: (100% - Projected %) \* Goal = Amount at Risk
      - **Days Remaining**: Number of days left in the current flight (not counting today).&#x20;
        **Daily**:
      - The bar graph displays data for the previous 14 days (not counting today).&#x20;
      - Each bar represents one day and shows the contrast between **Goal** and **Spent**.
    </td>
  </tr>
